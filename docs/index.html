CivicSuite / CivicComms

Explain city work without losing the source trail.

CivicComms v0.1.1 ships a foundation for source-readiness review, meeting summary outlines, ordinance explainers, newsletters, FAQ prompts, and audience-specific draft variants.

Shipping v0.1.1

What ships

  • Source-readiness checks.
  • Meeting summary draft outlines.
  • Ordinance explainer drafts.
  • Newsletter and FAQ scaffolds.
  • Audience variant drafts.
  • Public sample UI at /civiccomms.

What does not ship

CivicComms does not publish autonomously, create campaign or advocacy content, provide legal advice, certify translations, call live LLMs, post to social media, or replace a communications system of record.
